About Tweetwave

Tweetwave is a platform where users can submit their confessions, and they will be automatically tweeted to our account. This allows users to anonymously share their thoughts and feelings with the world.

To submit a confession, simply fill out the form on the homepage and click submit. Your confession will be tweeted out shortly after, with no information identifying you.
